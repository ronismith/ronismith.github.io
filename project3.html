MeshMash


Title: Messy


Medium: Digital 3D Model


Dimensions: 640x 480



I wanted to create a fantasy of a room gone completely chaotic and messy. I took inspiration from my dorm, and let my imagination run. My main challenge was in straddling the line between surrealism and realism.


When I undertook this challenge I focused mainly on the hanger object, which I got off of Poly. I inflated them, and put them on a hill of this goo. I added a hand poking out of the goo to give the scene a sense of impending danger. The door, behind the viewer in VR, is made to be smaller than the viewer and imply there is no possibility of escape.
